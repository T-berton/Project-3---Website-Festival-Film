About the Festival

The Boston Film Festival is a film festival that takes place every year. This year the festival takes place in Boston Public Garden from August 5 at 8:00 am until August 8 at 12:00 am, thanks to a special permission from the Mayor of Boston. Each day consists of three sessions (morning, afternoon and evening sessions). For each session, a different film will be shown.

The films chosen have been carefully selected to appeal to both adults and children.

The number of places is to 500 people, so we ask you to pre-register before coming. From 11am, the festival will have food and drink stalls every day. Tables will be available for eating and watching the film. Free parking will be provided by the Boston Public Garden.

This Festival is open to all and free of charge. So don't hesitate to pre-register, we're expecting a lot of you!
